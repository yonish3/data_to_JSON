Manufacturer: Wildling 
Model name: POLA 

Short description:
The yellow-beige Pola combines the best properties of cotton and linen.
In addition the integrated hemp-flax fleece insole covered in GOTS-certified
cotton ensures that you feet feel completely happy in this minimal shoe and can
stride carefree into the summer.
With the thin and flexible outsole you can feel the ground and can give your
feet freedom of movement.
The flat yellow cotton laces can be switched for other cotton or elastic laces,
available separately.
Please note:
Each individual shoe is a unique, handcrafted item with an upper made of a
natural material.
You may therefore find slight irregularities in texture and color. 

Long description:
Upper:
Fancy weave (66% cotton from certified organic sources, 34% linen from certified
organic sources) - yellow / beige
Lining / insole:
100% cotton from certified organic sources
Edging:
microfibre (100% polyamide) - sand
Outsole:
80% synthetic rubber with 50% recycled content, 20% cork;
2.5 mm (36 - 41) / 3.5 mm (42 - 48) - off-white
Additional information:
contains no products of animal origin
